Alejandro Huerta

Data Science Student In Autonomous University of Baja California

Second Semester

My passion for technology and computers began at the age of 14, when I first immersed myself in the world of video games. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a deeper curiosity about how games worked and the systems behind them. I began researching and learning about the tools and technologies involved, driven by a desire to understand their inner workings.

At 17, I took my first steps into web development by enrolling in a web design course during high school. This experience solidified my interest in the tech world and confirmed that my future would be in computing. Motivated by this realization, I decided to pursue a career related to software development to take my passion to the next level.

Out of all the paths available to me, the field of Data Science stood out the most. Its combination of technology, analytics, and real-world impact fascinated me. I am currently in the early stages of my Data Science studies, building a solid foundation and expanding my knowledge every day.
